# Koldarc — Google Business Profile Setup Pack

Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is the **single biggest factor** in ranking
for "mobile welding Knoxville" and showing up in the Google Map pack. This pack
is paste-ready — fill in the few `<…>` placeholders and you can be set up in
about 20 minutes.

Create/claim it at **https://business.google.com**.

---

## 1. Business type — set as a Service-Area Business (SAB)

When asked "Do you have a location customers can visit?" → **No**.
You are mobile, so you **hide your address** and instead list the areas you serve.
This is correct and expected for a mobile welder — do not list a storefront.

## 8. Reviews — the #1 differentiator

This is what separates the #1 result from the #6. Make asking automatic.

1. In GBP, grab your **review short link** (Home → "Get more reviews").
2. Text or email it to every customer the day the job is done.
3. Reply to every review (Google rewards engagement).

**Text template:**
```
Thanks for letting Koldarc handle that for you! If you were happy with the
work, a quick Google review really helps a small local business: <REVIEW LINK>
```

**Email template:**
```
Subject: Quick favor?

Hi <name>, thanks again for the business. If the repair held up like it should,
would you mind leaving a short Google review? It takes 30 seconds and makes a
big difference for us: <REVIEW LINK>

— Koldarc, Mobile Welding · Knoxville
```

Target: 10 reviews fast, then a steady trickle. Recent reviews mentioning
"mobile welding" and "Knoxville" are the most valuable.
